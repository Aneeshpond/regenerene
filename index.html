Technology

We plan to combine the strengths of both nanotechnology and graphene in order to combat degenerative diseases, and make cures available for everyone.

Stem Cells: Stem cells are unspecialized cells that have extraordinary ability to self renew. These cells can differentiate into many of the body's cells. Through the use of certain growth factors, we can control differentiation, and get whatever types of cells that we want.

Graphene: Graphene is an allotrope of carbon, which has exhibited extreme strength, unique surface properties, and versatile chemistry. It shows promise in the field of stem cells as a scaffold material.
